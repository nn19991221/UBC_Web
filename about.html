Since 1995, UBC has connected North American brands with reliable manufacturing — from product development to bulk production and logistics.

Who We Are

United Business Corporation (UBC) is a Los Angeles–founded apparel partner offering end-to-end services: sampling, product development, manufacturing, quality control, and shipping. We work with clients across North America to deliver consistent quality, on-time schedules, and transparent communication.

  • • Founded in 1995 in Los Angeles
  • • USA factory for quick-turn & small MOQ
  • • Global sourcing & cost optimization
  • • Development support (pattern, grading)
  • • Quality control & AQL inspections
  • • Logistics coordination & compliance

Milestones & Growth

  • 1995
    UBC founded in Los Angeles

    Started as a sourcing and manufacturing partner serving North American brands.

  • 2008
    Expanded development services

  • 2016
    USA facility upgrade

    Improved quick‑turn capabilities for small MOQs and fast replenishment.

  • 2021
    Quality & compliance focus

    Enhanced AQL inspection workflows and documentation for brand audits.

  • Today
    Global network, local service

    Dual‑track production across USA and overseas, balancing speed and cost.
